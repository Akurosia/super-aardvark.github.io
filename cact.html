Mini Cactpot Calculator

Instructions

  1. Begin by entering the revealed number on your Mini Cactpot card.
  2. Press enter or tab, and we will recommend one or more numbers to reveal next.
  3. Continue entering each number as you reveal it, and the recommendations will be updated.
  4. Once you have revealed all four numbers, we will display the average payout for each row, and highlight the best row to choose.

You can use these buttons if you're curious about situations that aren't allowed in the game (e.g. revealing more than four numbers). "Recommend" gives a recommendation for which number to reveal next. "Calculate" shows the expected payouts.
